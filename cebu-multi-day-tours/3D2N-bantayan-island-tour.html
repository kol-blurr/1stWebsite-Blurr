Escape to the Enchanting Shores of Bantayan Island for 3 Unforgettable Days (2 Nights)!

Embark on an extended journey through the captivating beauty of Bantayan Island with X&A Travel & Tours! This 3-day, 2-night adventure unveils pristine beaches, hidden caves, vibrant coral reefs, and the relaxed island charm of Bantayan.

Day 1: Unveiling Bantayan's Treasures

    ◉ Immerse in Nature: Delve into the lush greenery of the Mangrove Eco Park and appreciate the vital role of mangroves in the ecosystem.

    ◉ Paradise Found: Unwind on the pristine shores of Paradise Beach, a haven boasting powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. Relax on the beach, take a refreshing dip, or try your hand at water sports (optional).

    ◉ Unveiling Hidden Wonders: Explore the natural beauty of Ogtong Cave, a fascinating limestone formation with an underground freshwater pool. Discover the unique ecosystem and marvel at the stalactites and stalagmites.

    ◉ Island Vibes at Kota Sandbar: Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Kota Sandbar, a popular spot known for its stunning views and shallow turquoise waters. Enjoy delicious seafood (meal not included) and soak up the laid-back island vibes.

    ◉ Witnessing History: Explore the remnants of the German Ruins, a historical landmark offering a glimpse into Bantayan's past.

Day 2: Island Hopping Delights & Underwater Wonders

    ◉ Virgin Island Escape: Upgrade your experience with a visit to Virgin Island, a tiny sandbar offering breathtaking views and crystal-clear waters (additional cost applies).

    ◉ Snorkeling Paradise: Gear up for an underwater adventure at Hilantagaan Island. Explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish and diverse marine life.

Day 3: Relaxation & Departure

    ◉ Free Time at Leisure: Enjoy some free time to explore Bantayan at your own pace. Relax on the beach, shop for souvenirs, or indulge in optional activities like kayaking or exploring local villages.

    ◉ A Relaxed Departure: Check out of your accommodation, board the ferry back to Hagnaya, and enjoy a comfortable return journey to Cebu City or the airport.

A Seamless & Rewarding Experience:

    ◉ Comfortable & Convenient Travel: Travel in style with our exclusive air-conditioned transportation throughout the tour. Roundtrip ferry tickets, comfortable overnight accommodation, two-night breakfast, and boat rentals are also included.

    ◉ Knowledgeable Driver/Guide: Our friendly and informative driver/guide will be by your side throughout the tour, sharing insights about the island's history, culture, and hidden gems.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Fun (mostly): Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, environmental fees, and a hassle-free experience. Meals (except breakfast) are excluded, allowing you to explore the island's diverse culinary scene at your own expense.

    ◉ Flexibility: You can indulge in delicious meals, water sports activities, and additional island-hopping experiences at your own expense.
