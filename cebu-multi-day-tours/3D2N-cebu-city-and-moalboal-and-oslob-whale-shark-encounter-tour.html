Day 1: Unveiling Cebu City's Gems

    ◉ A Historical & Cultural Immersion: Delve into Cebu's rich past with visits to historical landmarks like Magellan's Cross, Santo Niño Church, Yap's Ancestral House, and the Cebu Heritage Monument. Discover the city's cultural influences through the Taoist Temple and the vibrant Sirao Flower Garden.

    ◉ Modern Delights: Explore the bustling business district and iconic landmarks like the Temple of Leah, a testament to love and devotion.

    ◉ Souvenir Shopping: Indulge in some retail therapy at the Shamrock Pasalubong Center, a haven for local delicacies and handcrafted souvenirs.

    ◉ A Culinary Adventure (Optional): Savor the distinct flavors of Cebu at the iconic House of Lechon (meal not included). Experience the city's signature roasted suckling pig dish.

Day 2: Moalboal's Marine Paradise & Adventure (Optional)

    ◉ A Glimpse into the Deep: Embark on a snorkeling adventure in Moalboal, renowned for its vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish and diverse marine life. Witness the incredible Sardine Run, a mesmerizing underwater spectacle (seasonal).

    ◉ Encountering Gentle Giants (Optional): Upgrade your experience with a visit to a marine sanctuary to swim responsibly alongside majestic whale sharks in Oslob (additional cost applies).

    ◉ Thrilling Adventure (Optional): Challenge yourself with an exciting canyoneering adventure in Kawasan Falls, surrounded by lush greenery and cascading waterfalls (additional cost applies).

Day 3: Unveiling Oslob & Beyond

    ◉ A Once-in-a-Lifetime Encounter: Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of whale sharks in Oslob, swimming responsibly alongside these gentle giants in their natural habitat.

    ◉ Island Paradise (Optional): Upgrade your experience with a visit to Sumilon Island, a tiny paradise boasting pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters (additional cost applies).

    ◉ A Spiritual Sojourn: Visit the Simala Church, a popular pilgrimage site known for its miraculous statue of the Virgin Mary.

    ◉ A Taste of Local Delights: Stop by the Carcar Pasalubong Center, a haven for local delicacies and souvenirs. Pick up unique gifts and treats to remember your Cebu adventure.

    ◉ Convenient Departure: Relax and enjoy a comfortable transfer back to the airport, concluding your unforgettable Cebu exploration.

A Hassle-Free & Memorable Experience:

    ◉ Comfortable & Convenient Travel: Travel in style with our exclusive air-conditioned transportation throughout the tour. Roundtrip airport transfers, comfortable overnight accommodation for two nights, and all boat fees are included.

    ◉ Knowledgeable Driver/Guide: Our friendly and informative driver/guide will be by your side throughout the tour, sharing insights and historical details about each location.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Fun (mostly): Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, environmental fees, and a hassle-free experience. Meals (except breakfast, which your accommodation may offer) are excluded, allowing you to explore Cebu's diverse culinary scene at your own pace.

    ◉ Flexibility: To personalize your adventure, you can add exciting experiences like canyoneering, a whale shark encounter in Oslob (additional cost applies), and a visit to Sumilon Island (additional cost applies).
