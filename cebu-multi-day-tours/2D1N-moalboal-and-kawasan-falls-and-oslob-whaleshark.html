Experience Cebu's Must-Sees: Moalboal, Kawasan Falls & Whale Sharks (2D1N)!

    ◉ Dive into Moalboal's Wonders: Explore Moalboal, a diver's paradise! Witness the mesmerizing Sardine Run, a breathtaking underwater spectacle of millions of sardines swimming in unison. Encounter majestic sea turtles and experience the vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish.

    ◉ Kawasan Falls Escape: Immerse yourself in the cool, cascading waters of Kawasan Falls, a multi-tiered natural wonder surrounded by lush greenery. Enjoy a complimentary meal and soak up the refreshing atmosphere.

    ◉ Canyoneering Adventure (Optional): Embark on an exhilarating canyoneering adventure (additional cost applies). Trek through the lush rainforest, rappel down waterfalls and discover hidden swimming holes.

Day 2: Encountering Whalesharks & Cebu Gems

    ◉ A Once-in-a-Lifetime Encounter: Witness the gentle giants of the sea at Oslob! Swim responsibly alongside majestic whale sharks, the world's largest fish species.

    Island Paradise (Optional): Upgrade your experience with a visit to Sumilon Island, a tiny paradise boasting pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters (additional cost applies).

    Spiritual Sojourn: Visit the Simala Church, a popular pilgrimage site known for its miraculous statue of the Virgin Mary.

    Cebu Delights: Stop by the Carcar Pasalubong Center, a haven for local delicacies and souvenirs. Pick up unique gifts and treats to remember your Cebu adventure.

    Convenient Departure: Relax and enjoy a comfortable transfer back to the airport, concluding your unforgettable Cebu exploration.
