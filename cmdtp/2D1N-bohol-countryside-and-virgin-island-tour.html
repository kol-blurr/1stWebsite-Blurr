Experience Bohol's Treasures & Virgin Island Paradise in 2 Days (All-Inclusive)!

    ◉ A Journey Through Time: Immerse yourself in history at the Blood Compact Shrine and Baclayon Church, uncovering Bohol's fascinating past.

    ◉ Encounter the Adorable Tarsiers: Get up close to the world's smallest primates at a dedicated sanctuary and learn about their conservation efforts.

    ◉ Nature's Majesty Awaits: Be mesmerized by the awe-inspiring Chocolate Hills, a geological marvel. Explore viewpoints and capture breathtaking panoramic photos.

    ◉ Explore Nature's Wonders: Immerse yourself in the vibrant Butterfly Garden and the Man-Made Forest, witnessing environmental efforts and natural beauty.

    ◉ Unveil Local Delights (Optional): Savor delicious Filipino cuisine aboard a floating restaurant on the Loboc River (not included).

    ◉ Uncover Local Legends (Optional): Encounter a majestic python at a designated viewing area and learn about its role in the ecosystem.

    ◉ Hollywood Meets Bohol: Explore the quirky Bohollywood Sikatuna's Mirror of the World, featuring miniature replicas of iconic landmarks.

Day 2: Island Hopping Adventure & Virgin Island Paradise

    ◉ Snorkeling Paradise: Gear up for an underwater adventure at the Fish & Turtle Sanctuary in Balicasag Island. Witness a dazzling array of colorful fish and encounter majestic sea turtles.

    ◉ Virgin Island Escape: Unwind on the pristine sandbar of Virgin Island, a tiny paradise offering breathtaking views and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Relax on the beach, take a refreshing dip, or soak up the sun-drenched scenery.

    ◉ Free Time & Departure: Enjoy some free time to explore at your own pace before departing for Tagbilaran Airport or returning to Cebu City.

A Hassle-Free & Memorable Experience:

    ◉ Comfortable & Convenient Travel: Travel in style with our exclusive air-conditioned transportation. Roundtrip ferry tickets, seaport transfers, and comfortable overnight accommodation are also included.

    ◉ Your Knowledgeable Guide: Our friendly and informative driver/guide will be with you throughout the tour, sharing insights and historical details about each location.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Fun: Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, boat fees, snorkeling gear, and a hassle-free experience.
