Embark on a Spiritual Journey to Simala Shrine with X&A Travel & Tours!

Experience the profound serenity and spiritual significance of the Simala Shrine, also known as the Monastery of the Holy Eucharist, on this captivating Cebu tour. This renowned pilgrimage site, dedicated to the miraculous Virgin of Lindogon, attracts devotees from all corners of the globe seeking blessings and spiritual renewal.

Explore the Shrine & Embrace the Culture:

    ◉ Simala Shrine: Immerse yourself in the sacred atmosphere of the Simala Shrine. Learn about the history of the miraculous Virgin Mary statue and witness the unwavering faith of devotees. Participate in a candle lighting ceremony and light a candle to represent your hopes and prayers.

    ◉ Carcar Pasalubong Center: Indulge in a delightful cultural experience at the vibrant Carcar Pasalubong Center. Discover a treasure trove of local delicacies and handcrafted souvenirs, the perfect way to remember your Cebu adventure. Sample the region's famous chicharon (deep-fried pork rinds) and explore the diverse selection of locally-made products.

    ◉ Taboan Market (Dried Fish): Immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere of Taboan Market, renowned for its impressive selection of dried fish and other local specialties. Witness the traditional drying techniques and explore the vast array of seafood options.
