Unwind on Pristine Shores & Dive into Culture with X&A Travel & Tours!

    ◉ Island Hopping Adventure: Embark on a thrilling island hopping adventure, cruising the crystal-clear waters of Bantayan. Explore hidden coves, discover secluded beaches, and soak up the sun-drenched scenery.

    ◉ Omagieca Camp Sawi: Delve into the local heritage with a visit to Omagieca Camp Sawi, a historical landmark showcasing remnants of Spanish colonial architecture. Learn about the island's fascinating past.

    ◉ Cliffside Thrills (Optional): For the adventurous, take the plunge at the cliff jumping spot (availability depends on sea conditions). Feel the adrenaline rush as you leap into the refreshing turquoise waters.

    ◉ Paradise Found: Unwind on the pristine shores of Paradise Beach, a haven boasting powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. Relax on the beach, take a refreshing dip, or try your hand at water sports (optional).

    ◉ Unveiling Hidden Wonders: Explore the natural beauty of Ogtong Cave, a fascinating limestone formation with an underground freshwater pool. Discover the unique ecosystem and marvel at the stalactites and stalagmites.

    ◉ Kota Beach Bliss: Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Kota Beach, a popular spot known for its lively beachfront restaurants and local shops. Enjoy delicious seafood (meal not included) and soak up the island vibes.

    ◉ Virgin Island Escape (Optional): Upgrade your experience with a visit to Virgin Island, a tiny sandbar offering stunning views and pristine waters (additional cost applies).

A Seamless & Rewarding Experience:

    ◉ Early Start, Full Day Fun: Begin your adventure with a convenient airport transfer at 3:00 AM, maximizing your time on the island.

    ◉ Hassle-Free Travel: Enjoy comfortable and efficient transportation throughout the day with our exclusive air-conditioned vehicles.

    ◉ Unveiling the Island: Our knowledgeable guide will be by your side, sharing insights about the island's history, culture, and hidden gems.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Exploration: Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, environmental fees, roundtrip ferry tickets, and boat rentals for island hopping.
