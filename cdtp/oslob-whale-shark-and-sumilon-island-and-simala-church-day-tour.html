Experience the Magic of Cebu on an Unforgettable Day Tour with X&A Travel & Tours!

    ◉ Encounter Gentle Giants: Thrill to a once-in-a-lifetime experience swimming alongside majestic whale sharks in Oslob. Witness these awe-inspiring creatures glide gracefully through the crystal-clear waters, creating lasting memories.

    ◉ Island Paradise Awaits: Escape to the idyllic Sumilon Island, a haven boasting a stunning sandbar and crystal-clear waters. Relax on the pristine beach, soak up the sun, and enjoy swimming or snorkeling in the vibrant coral reefs (entrance fees not included).

    ◉ Unveil Oslob's Rich History: Immerse yourself in the local heritage with a visit to Oslob Cuartel, a historical landmark showcasing the town's vibrant past. (Optional)

    ◉ Seek Spiritual Solace: Journey to the revered Simala Church, a popular pilgrimage site dedicated to the miraculous Virgin of Lindogon. Witness the unwavering faith of devotees and participate in a candle-lighting ceremony (optional) to offer your prayers and hopes.

    ◉ Treasure Hunting (Optional): Indulge in a delightful shopping experience at Carcar Pasalubong Center, a treasure trove of local delicacies and handcrafted souvenirs. Take home a piece of Cebu to remember your adventure.

A Hassle-Free & Memorable Experience:

    ◉ Comfortable Travels: Enjoy a convenient and comfortable journey throughout the day with our air-conditioned transportation.

    ◉ Experienced Driver/Guide: Our friendly and knowledgeable driver/guide will be your companion throughout the tour, sharing insights about the locations and ensuring a smooth experience.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Fun: Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, environmental fees, boat fees, and necessary gear for the whale shark encounter.

    ◉ Convenient Pick-Up & Drop-Off: Enjoy the ease of pick-up and drop-off directly at your hotel or residence within Cebu City, allowing you to unwind and savor the adventure.
