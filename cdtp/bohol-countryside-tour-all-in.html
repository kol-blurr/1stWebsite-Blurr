Discover the Enchanting Beauty of Bohol in a Day with X&A Travel & Tours!

    ◉ A Step Back in Time: Immerse yourself in history at the Blood Compact Shrine, a significant landmark commemorating an early treaty between Spanish and Filipino leaders.

    ◉ Architectural Marvel: Marvel at the neoclassical beauty of the Baclayon Church, one of the oldest churches in the Philippines, with its impressive facade and intricate stonework.

    ◉ Meeting the Endearing Tarsiers: Get up close to the world's smallest primates, the adorable tarsiers, at a dedicated sanctuary. Observe their unique features and learn about their conservation efforts.

    ◉ A Culinary Adventure (Optional): Enjoy a delightful lunch experience aboard a floating restaurant (not included in the tour price). Savor delicious Filipino cuisine while cruising along the Loboc River, surrounded by stunning scenery.

    ◉ Nature's Majesty: Be mesmerized by the awe-inspiring Chocolate Hills, a geological marvel consisting of over 1,200 cone-shaped hills. Explore viewpoints and capture breathtaking panoramic photos.

    ◉ A Flutter of Colors: Immerse yourself in a vibrant display of nature at the Butterfly Garden. Witness a dazzling array of colorful butterflies flitting amongst the flowers and learn about their vital role in the ecosystem.

    ◉ A Sustainable Marvel: Explore the Man-Made Forest, a testament to environmental rehabilitation efforts. Witness towering mahogany trees and appreciate the vital role of reforestation.

    ◉ Unveiling a Local Mystery: Encounter a majestic python at a designated viewing area (optional). Learn about these fascinating creatures and their role in the local ecosystem.

A Seamless & Rewarding Experience:

    ◉ Convenient Transportation: Travel comfortably and efficiently throughout Bohol with our exclusive air-conditioned transportation. Roundtrip ferry tickets from Cebu and seaport transfers are also included, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

    ◉ Knowledgeable Driver/Guide: Our friendly and informative driver/guide will be by your side throughout the tour, sharing insightful stories and historical details about each location.

    ◉ All-Inclusive Experience: Relax and focus on creating memories - your tour includes all entrance fees, ensuring a convenient and stress-free exploration of Bohol's wonders.

    ◉ Flexibility: Enjoy the option to include a delightful lunch experience aboard a floating restaurant (additional cost applies).
